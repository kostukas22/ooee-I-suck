The quest for Boba

One time not so long ago... I was at a hackathon. In the middle of it they had a thing where if you built a website you could get boba. You needed to theme your website around a side quest you have done, but I could not think of anything.

The solution

I realised the act of making a website about a side quest was a side quest its self. Because of this realization I decided to write about the process of writing about this.
